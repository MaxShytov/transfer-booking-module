# Mobile App User Guide

Welcome to the 8Move Transfer mobile application guide. This comprehensive documentation will help you understand all features and functionality of the customer-facing mobile app.

## Overview

The 8Move Transfer mobile app is your gateway to booking professional transfer services. Available for iOS and Android, the app provides a streamlined booking experience with transparent pricing and multi-language support.

## Quick Start

1. **Download** the app from App Store or Google Play
2. **Register** your account with email and password
3. **Start Booking** by tapping "Book Your Transfer"
4. **Follow the wizard** through 5 simple steps
5. **Confirm** and receive your booking reference
